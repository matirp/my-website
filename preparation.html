Step-by-Step Preparation Process

  1. Add Aloe Vera Gel to the beaker.
  2. Pour Hydrogen Peroxide to disinfect the mixture.
  3. Add Distilled Water for dilution.
  4. Mix in Glycerol to maintain moisture.
  5. Add Ethanol (IPA) as the main sanitizing agent.
  6. Stir the solution well.
  7. Pour into bottles for final use.
